## Install

1. Download latest `Hayya-v*.apk` from [Releases](https://github.com/karimVentus/Private-Prayer/releases).
2. Enable “Install unknown apps” for your browser/files app.
3. Open APK and install.

Or build locally: `./gradlew assembleDebug` → `app/build/outputs/apk/debug/app-debug.apk`
